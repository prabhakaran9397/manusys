About Us

Established in the year 1978 as Society of Industrial and Production Engineers (SIPE) for empowerment of students of production background. Later in the year 1996 it was renamed as ASSOCIATION OF MANUFACTURING ENGINEERS for the young minds of manufacturing division. Since then it has proved to be an effective contributor in expansion of knowledge. This year AME completes its 20th anniversary.

AME has organized various Guest Lectures by Notable personalities for students of Department of Manufacturing Engineering and College of Engineering Guindy. It also focuses on development of management and technical skills of students by organizing National level technical symposium under the banner MANUSYS. MANUSYS aims in forging the knowledge of students towards excellency.

Following MANUSYS we are organizing “WORKSHOP WEEK” in order to propagate practical approach to the engineering students and to enrich them with knowledge. It mainly focuses on Automotive assembly and Mechatronics. AME will always be a path finder for the students to achieve their dreams.
